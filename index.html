Lili Bo is a lecturer in School of Information Engineering at Yangzhou University. He received his bachelor degree and master degree in Computer Science and Technology from Qufu Normal University in 2013 and 2015, respectively. Then, she joined School of Computer Science and Technology at China University of Mining and Technology, and received her Ph.D in 2019. Her research interests include software testing, software maintenance and evolution, software vulnerability analysis and testing, software data analytics, intelligence analysis in software engineering, etc. She has published about eight papers in the international journals and conferences. She is a CCF and ACM/IEEE member.

Research:
Software testing
Software vulnerability analysis and testing
Software maintenance and evolution
Software data analysis and explanation
Intelligence analysis in software engineering
